## Interpretation

**0% = confirmed data bottleneck.**

All reward signals in the experience buffer are `−0.01` (step penalty, no level completion).
TTT on this experience reduces loss on the negative-reward pattern — it trains the model
to expect every step to cost −0.01. There's no positive gradient direction to exploit.

MCTS with 25 simulations from random-initialized weights explores uniformly; without a
meaningful value function, PUCT selects near-randomly. Scope 2 cross-level updates have
no past level history to draw on (always starting from scratch for the new level).

The diagnostic threshold is met: Stage 3 TTT with data-quality-limited weights = 0%,
indistinguishable from the random baseline.
